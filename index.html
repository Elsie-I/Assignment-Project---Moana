Disney's Moana

There was once this Demi-God who stole a piece of jewelry from Mother Nature (who turned into a lava monster btw), then loses it into the ocean. Fast forward a couple decades, the ocean chooses a little girl, because she happened to save a turtle, and makes her the keeper of this jewel. Now it's her responsibility to find this Demi-God and have him return the jewelry before life ends. Fin!
